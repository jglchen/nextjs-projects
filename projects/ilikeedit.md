This is a commercially established social network site, preferably on news exchanges and sharings. In the site we use **MySQL** as the storage engine, **PHP** for the back end, and **React.js** for the front end. 
In the current stage we are developing the mobile apps for iOS and Android with **React Native**. In addition **responsive web CSS designs** are adopted for the site, that makes all the pages look good and user-friendly on all devices with a single set of programs.

Users can continuously follow up **real-time updated hot news feed** based on their use experiences and subscriptions. They are freely to **share, exchange, and publish news** to the public or friends. Furthermore users can **share information privately with identified friends only** through our private messaging mechanism.
